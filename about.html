ABOUT US

BENEVOLENT ORGANIZATION OF ALL NATIONS

The BENEVOLENT ORGANIZATION OF ALL NATIONS (BOOAN) is a non-profit, non-governmental organization (NGO) committed dedicated to tackling some of the world's most pressing challenges while fostering empowerment and operating at the forefront of humanitarian and social development services.

BOOAN engages distinctive systems applications to drive policies, strategies and solutions with servicing capabilities ahead of the curve in collaboration within an extensive network to leverage resources, expertise, and collective action.

Operating in sync with international standards of performance and accountability, prioritizing unique needs, streamlining resources, and driving balanced stewardship within the ever-evolving global landscape is at the heart of the BOOAN endeavor.

MISSION

BOOAN upholds the Mission of creating a high-performance organization with the most effective strategies and pool of resources to enhance local and international development collaborations towards executing sustainable and comprehensive development initiatives.

VISION

BOOAN envisions a world sustainably fit to provide the decency of being for all creatures.

Purpose and Core Values

BOOAN is committed to a working culture driven on the principles of social justice and balance, empowerment and equity, efficiency and sustainability, inclusion and collaborations.

Love

Empathizing with and recognizing the inherent dignity of every person in attaining a better life.

Value

Empowering people and communities to create value for a better thriving world.

Sustainability

Striving for inclusion, promoting diversity and equality, transparency, integrity, and accountability, as well as fair and ethical relationships with our stakeholders towards realizing lasting legacy.

Strategic Goals of

BENEVOLENT ORGANIZATION OF ALL NATIONS

  • To render humanitarian and charitable support to the less privileged and vulnerable in society
  • To promote social welfare and security and the general wellbeing of all creatures
  • To enhance all-round socio-cultural, political and economic empowerment
  • To help pool stakeholders and resources towards the fulfillment of developmental outcomes
  • The build the capacity of development interests
Strategic Areas of Focus

Social Welfare and Development

Revolving around improving the well-being, quality of life, and overall development of individuals and communities, particularly those facing socio-economic challenges

  • Education: driving education as a powerful tool for social transformation towards while helping to increase access to quality education and promoting lifelong learning opportunities.
  • Healthcare Access: working to improve healthcare infrastructure, provide medical support services, and raise awareness about health issues, ensuring that communities have the care they need.
  • Poverty Alleviation: implementing sustainable poverty alleviation programs that empower individuals with income-generating skills, microfinance initiatives, and livelihood support.
  • Gender Equality: advancing gender equality and women's empowerment while helping to combat gender-based violence, promote women's rights, and create opportunities for women in education and the workforce.
  • Community Development: engaging with communities to identify their unique needs and aspirations while fostering a sense of ownership and self-reliance.

Economic Growth and Development

Create opportunities for individuals and communities to break the cycle of poverty, achieve financial independence, and contribute to their economies.

  • Entrepreneurship Development: Providing support services and fostering local enterprises to drive economic growth.
  • Training and Capacity Building:Training and development of individuals and communities for empowerment.
  • Financial Literacy: Carrying out workshops and facilitating resources to teach individuals, households and communities about budgeting, savings, and investments.
  • Access to Markets:Facilitating access to markets for local products and crafts to boost income, promote cultural preservation and sustainable practices.

Natural Resource Conservation and Management

Conservation of natural resources while driving sustainable development and supporting the role that the environment plays in well-being of present and future generations.

  • Sustainable Agriculture: : Promoting sustainable farming practices that reduce environmental impact, conserve soil and water, and increase crop yields towards ensuring food security and preservation of natural resources.
  • Renewable Energy:Facilitating transitioning to renewable energy sources while helping to reduce reliance on fossil fuels and mitigating climate change.
  • Biodiversity Conservation: Protecting biodiversity for ecological balance involving reforestation efforts, habitat restoration, and wildlife conservation.
  • Environmental and Disaster Management:Advancing environmental management and helping to provide emergency and disaster management support services.
